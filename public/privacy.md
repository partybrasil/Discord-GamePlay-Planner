# Privacy Policy for Discord GamePlay Planner

**Effective Date:** January 6, 2026

## 1. Information We Collect
We do not store any personal data permanently.
- **User ID & Username:** Used temporarily during the session to identify participants.
- **Game Data:** Whiteboard drawings are synced in real-time but wiped when the session ends (unless exported).

## 2. Data Usage
Data is used solely for the functionality of the tactical whiteboard (syncing strokes and cursors).

## 3. Contact
For questions, contact the developer via Discord.
